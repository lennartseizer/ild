2) Random Intercept Mixed-Effects-Model

$$ \text{Level 1: } y_{ij} = \color{blue}\beta_{0j} \color{black}+ \color{blue}\beta_{1}\color{gray}w_{ij} \color{black}+ \color{red}\varepsilon_{ij} $$ $$ \text{Level 2: } \color{blue}\beta_{0j} \color{black}= \color{blue}\gamma_{00} \color{black}+ \color{blue}\gamma_{01}\color{gray}b_{j} \color{black}+ \color{red}u_{0j} $$
$$ \begin{align*} \text{Complete: } y_{ij} = \color{blue}\gamma_{00}\color{black}+\color{blue}\gamma_{01}\color{gray}b_{j}\color{black}+\color{red}u_{0j} \color{black}+\color{blue}\beta_{1}\color{gray}w_{ij}\color{black}+\color{red}\varepsilon_{ij} \end{align*} $$

3) Random Slope Mixed-Effects-Model

$$ \text{Level 1: } y_{ij} = \color{blue}\beta_{0j} \color{black}+ \color{blue}\beta_{1j}\color{gray}w_{ij} \color{black}+ \color{red}\varepsilon_{ij} $$ $$ \text{Level 2: } \color{blue}\beta_{0j} \color{black}= \color{blue}\gamma_{00} \color{black}+ \color{blue}\gamma_{01}\color{gray}b_{j} $$ $$ \text{Level 2: } \color{blue}\beta_{1j} \color{black}= \color{blue}\gamma_{10} \color{black}+ \color{red}u_{1j} $$

5) Full Random Mixed-Effects-Model with AR(1)

$$ \text{Level 1: } y_{ij} = \color{blue}\beta_{0j} \color{black}+ \color{blue}\beta_{1j}\color{gray}w_{ij} \color{black}+ \color{red}\varepsilon_{ij} $$ $$ \text{Level 1: } \color{red}\varepsilon_{ij} \color{black}= \color{blue}\rho\color{red}\varepsilon_{(i-1)j} \color{black}+ \color{red}e_{ij} $$ $$ \text{Level 2: } \color{blue}\beta_{0j} \color{black}= \color{blue}\gamma_{00} \color{black}+ \color{blue}\gamma_{01}\color{gray}b_{j} \color{black}+ \color{red}u_{0j} $$ $$ \text{Level 2: } \color{blue}\beta_{1j} \color{black}= \color{blue}\gamma_{10} \color{black}+ \color{red}u_{1j} $$
$$ \begin{align*} \text{Complete: } y_{ij} &= \color{blue}\gamma_{00} \color{black} + \color{blue}\gamma_{01}\color{gray}b_{j} \color{black} + \color{red}u_{0j} \color{black}+\color{blue}\gamma_{10}\color{gray}w_{ij} \color{black}+ \color{red}u_{1j}\color{gray}w_{ij} \\ &\quad + \color{blue}\rho\color{red}\varepsilon_{(i-1)j} \color{black}+ \color{red}e_{ij} \end{align*} $$

7) Three-level Full Random Mixed-Effects-Model with AR(1) & Cross-level Moderation

$$ \text{Level 1: } y_{ijg} = \color{blue}\beta_{0jg} \color{black}+ \color{blue}\beta_{1jg}\color{gray}w_{ijg} \color{black}+ \color{red}\varepsilon_{ijg} $$ $$ \text{Level 1: } \color{red}\varepsilon_{ijg} \color{black}= \color{blue}\rho\color{red}\varepsilon_{(i-1)jg} \color{black}+ \color{red}e_{ijg} $$ $$ \text{Level 2: } \color{blue}\beta_{0jg} \color{black}= \color{blue}\gamma_{00g} \color{black}+ \color{blue}\gamma_{01}\color{gray}b_{jg} \color{black}+ \color{red}u_{0jg} $$ $$ \text{Level 2: } \color{blue}\beta_{1jg} \color{black}= \color{blue}\gamma_{10g} \color{black}+ \color{blue}\gamma_{11}\color{gray}b_{jg} \color{black}+ \color{red}u_{1jg} $$ $$ \text{Level 3: } \color{blue}\gamma_{00g} \color{black}= \color{blue}\delta_{000} \color{black}+ \color{red}v_{0g} $$ $$ \text{Level 3: } \color{blue}\gamma_{10g} \color{black}= \color{blue}\delta_{100} \color{black}+ \color{red}v_{1g} $$
$$ \begin{align*} \text{Complete: } y_{ijg} &= \color{blue}\delta_{000} \color{black}+ \color{red}v_{0g} \color{black} + \color{blue}\gamma_{01}\color{gray}b_{jg} \color{black} + \color{red}u_{0jg} \color{black}+\color{blue}\delta_{100}\color{gray}w_{ijg}\color{black}+ \color{red}v_{1g}\color{gray}w_{ijg} \\ &\quad \color{black}+ \color{blue}\gamma_{11}\color{gray}b_{jg}\color{gray}w_{ijg} \color{black}+\color{red}u_{1jg}\color{gray}w_{ijg} \color{black}+\color{blue}\rho\color{red}\varepsilon_{(i-1)jg} \color{black}+ \color{red}e_{ijg} \end{align*} $$
